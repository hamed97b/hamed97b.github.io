About

I am an assistant professor and researcher in Mathematics at the National Higher School of Mathematics (NHSM), Alger, Algeria. My research sits at the intersection of mathematical analysis, numerical methods, and mathematical physics, with a particular focus on the stability of thermoelastic systems.

I have contributed to the theoretical and numerical study of Bresse, Timoshenko, and Lord–Shulman systems, publishing in internationally recognised journals including Mathematical Methods in the Applied Sciences, Computers & Mathematics with Applications, and Applicable Analysis.

Alongside my research, I am committed to teaching and mentoring students at both bachelor and master levels, covering topics from foundational analysis to advanced stochastic optimal control and computer algebra systems.
